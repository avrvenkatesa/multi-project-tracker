Issues & Action Items

Track problems and manage tasks with Issues and Action Items.

1 Issues vs Action Items

Issues

Problems, bugs, or concerns that need resolution

  • • Category-based organization
  • • Phase tracking
  • • Component assignment
  • • Risk assessment

Action Items

Specific tasks or activities to complete

  • • Simple task management
  • • Quick creation
  • • Owner assignment
  • • Due date tracking

2 Creating Issues

  1. Open your project
  2. Click "New Issue" button
  3. Fill in the required fields:
    • Title: Clear, concise description of the problem
    • Description: Detailed explanation with context
    • Status: To Do, In Progress, In Review, or Resolved
    • Priority: Critical, High, Medium, or Low
    • Category: Type based on project template
    • Phase: Current stage in workflow
    • Component: System or area affected
    • Assigned To: Team member responsible
    • Due Date: Target completion date
  4. Click "Create Issue"

Pro Tip:

Include relevant details like error messages, screenshots descriptions, or steps to reproduce in the description field.

3 Creating Action Items

  1. Open your project
  2. Click "New Action Item" button
  3. Fill in the details:
    • Title: What needs to be done
    • Description: Additional context
    • Status: Pending, In Progress, Blocked, or Completed
    • Priority: Critical, High, Medium, or Low
    • Assigned To: Team member responsible
    • Due Date: Deadline
  4. Click "Create Action Item"

4 Editing Items

Update issues and action items as work progresses:

  1. Click on the item to view details
  2. Click the "Edit" button
  3. Modify any field as needed
  4. Click "Save Changes"

Status Updates: Keep status current to reflect actual progress. This helps team visibility and reporting accuracy.

Quick Status Change:

Use the Kanban board to drag and drop items between columns for quick status updates.

5 Item Relationships

Link related items to track dependencies and connections:

Blocks

Issue A blocks Issue B - B cannot proceed until A is resolved

Parent/Child

Break down large issues into smaller sub-issues for better tracking

Related

Items that are connected but not dependent on each other

The AI Meeting Analysis feature can automatically detect and create these relationships from meeting transcripts!

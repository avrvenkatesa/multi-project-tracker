Team Management

Invite team members, assign roles, and manage project access.

1 Inviting Team Members

Project Managers and Admins can invite new members:

  1. Open your project
  2. Click the "Team" button
  3. Click "Invite Team Member"
  4. Enter the team member's email address
  5. Select their role (see Roles section below)
  6. Add an optional personal message
  7. Click "Send Invitation"

Email Notification:

The invitee will receive an email with a link to accept or decline the invitation.

2 Accepting Invitations

When you receive a project invitation:

  1. Check your email for the invitation
  2. Click the invitation link in the email
  3. Log in if prompted
  4. Review the project details and your assigned role
  5. Click "Accept" or "Decline"

You can also view pending invitations by clicking "My Invitations" in the main navigation.

3 Team Roles

Each team member has a role that determines their permissions:

Admin

Full project control including deletion

  • ✓ Create, edit, delete all items
  • ✓ Manage team members
  • ✓ Modify project settings
  • ✓ Delete project

Manager

Can manage team and moderate content

  • ✓ Create, edit, delete all items
  • ✓ Invite and manage team members
  • ✓ Generate reports
  • ✗ Cannot delete project

Member

Regular team contributor

  • ✓ Create and edit own items
  • ✓ Comment on all items
  • ✓ View all project data
  • ✗ Cannot manage team

Viewer

Read-only access for stakeholders

  • ✓ View all items and comments
  • ✓ View dashboard and reports
  • ✗ Cannot create or edit
  • ✗ Cannot comment

See Roles & Permissions for complete details.

4 Managing Team Members

Update member roles and permissions:

  1. Go to the Team page
  2. Find the team member
  3. Click the "Edit" or gear icon
  4. Update their role
  5. Click "Save Changes"

Permission Required:

Only Managers and Admins can modify team member roles.

5 Removing Team Members

Remove a member from the project:

  1. Go to the Team page
  2. Find the team member to remove
  3. Click "Remove"
  4. Confirm the action

Important:

Removed members lose all access to the project immediately. Their created items and comments remain in the project.

Email Notifications

Configure when and how you receive email notifications for project activity.

1 Email Notifications Overview

Stay informed about project activity with customizable email notifications:

  • Control which events trigger notifications
  • Set email delivery frequency (instant, daily digest, weekly digest)
  • Enable test emails to verify your settings
  • Unsubscribe from all notifications with one click

Access Settings:

Click "Settings" in the main navigation to configure your notification preferences.

2 Notification Types

Choose which events trigger notifications:

@Mentions

Receive notifications when someone @mentions you in a comment

Recommended: ✓ Enabled

Assignments

Get notified when issues or action items are assigned to you

Recommended: ✓ Enabled

Status Changes

Be informed when items you created or are assigned to change status

Recommended: ✓ Enabled

Project Invitations

Receive emails when invited to join new projects

Recommended: ✓ Enabled (cannot be disabled)

3 Configure Settings

Customize your notification preferences:

  1. Click "Settings" in the main navigation
  2. Toggle each notification type on or off
  3. Select your preferred email frequency
  4. Click "Send Test Email" to verify (optional)
  5. Click "Save Preferences"

Test Email:

Send yourself a test notification to confirm your email address is correct and emails are being delivered.

4 Email Frequency Options

Control how often you receive emails:

Instant

Receive emails immediately as events occur

Best for: Active projects, urgent notifications

Daily Digest

Get one email per day summarizing all activity

Best for: Reducing email volume, regular updates

Weekly Digest

Receive a weekly summary every Monday

Best for: Long-term projects, high-level oversight

5 Unsubscribe from Notifications

Stop receiving all email notifications:

From Settings Page:

  1. Go to Settings → Notifications
  2. Click "Unsubscribe from All"
  3. Confirm your choice

From Any Email:

  1. Scroll to the bottom of any notification email
  2. Click the "Unsubscribe" link
  3. Confirm on the web page

Re-subscribe Anytime:

You can re-enable notifications at any time from your Settings page.

AI Meeting Analysis

Automatically extract action items and issues from meeting transcripts using AI.

1 AI Meeting Analysis Overview

The AI Meeting Analysis feature uses GPT-3.5-Turbo to:

  • Extract Issues: Identify problems, concerns, and risks discussed
  • Extract Action Items: Find tasks, assignments, and follow-ups
  • Detect Status Updates: Recognize updates to existing items
  • Identify Relationships: Discover blocking, parent-child, and related connections
  • Smart Matching: Match status updates to existing items with confidence scoring

Two-Phase Processing:

Phase 1 extracts new items. Phase 2 detects status updates and matches them to existing work.

2 Uploading Meeting Transcripts

Upload and analyze meeting transcripts:

  1. Navigate to your project
  2. Click "AI Analysis" button
  3. Click "Upload Transcript"
  4. Paste or upload your meeting transcript text
  5. Add a meeting title and date (optional)
  6. Click "Analyze Transcript"
  7. Wait while the AI processes the content (usually 10-30 seconds)

Transcript Quality Tips:

Better transcripts = better results. Include speaker names, timestamps, and full context for best AI extraction accuracy.

3 Review Queue

After analysis, review the extracted items:

New Items

Issues and action items ready to be added to your project

Status Updates

Updates to existing items with confidence scores and suggested matches

Relationships

Detected connections between items (blocking, parent-child, related)

Review each item carefully before accepting to ensure accuracy.

4 Accepting Items

Add AI-extracted items to your project:

  1. Review the extracted item details
  2. Edit title, description, or other fields as needed
  3. For status updates, review the matched item and confidence score
  4. Click "Accept" to add the item
  5. Or click "Reject" to discard

Bulk Actions:

Use "Accept All" for high-confidence extractions or review individually for quality control.

Comments & @Mentions

Collaborate with your team using comments and @mention notifications.

1 Adding Comments

Add comments to issues or action items:

  1. Open an issue or action item
  2. Scroll to the Comments section
  3. Type your comment in the text area
  4. Click "Add Comment"

Comments appear in chronological order with timestamps and author names.

Comment Best Practices:

Be specific, constructive, and include relevant context. Use comments to provide updates, ask questions, or share findings.

2 @Mentions

Notify specific team members using @mentions:

  1. While typing a comment, type @
  2. An autocomplete dropdown appears with team members
  3. Type to filter or click to select a team member
  4. The mention is highlighted in your comment
  5. Submit the comment

Real-Time Autocomplete:

Start typing a name after @ and the list filters automatically. Press Enter or click to insert the mention.

Mentioned users receive email notifications (if enabled in their settings).

3 Mention Notifications

When someone @mentions you:

  • You receive an email notification (if enabled)
  • The notification includes the comment text and context
  • Click the "View Comment" link to navigate directly to the item
  • The specific comment is highlighted when you arrive

4 Markdown Support

Format comments using Markdown syntax:

**Bold Text**

*Italic Text*

`Code snippet`

- Bullet point

1. Numbered list

[Link text](url)

Markdown is rendered when the comment is displayed, making it easy to create structured, readable comments.

5 Editing & Deleting Comments

Modify or remove your own comments:

Editing:

  1. Find your comment
  2. Click the "Edit" button
  3. Modify the text
  4. Click "Save"

Deleting:

  1. Find your comment
  2. Click the "Delete" button
  3. Confirm the deletion

Permissions:

You can only edit/delete your own comments. Managers and Admins can delete any comment.

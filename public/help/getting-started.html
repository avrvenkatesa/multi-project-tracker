Getting Started with Multi-Project Tracker

Welcome to Multi-Project Tracker! This guide will help you get up and running in minutes.

1 Logging In

To access Multi-Project Tracker, you'll need an account:

  1. Navigate to the login page
  2. Enter your email and password
  3. Click "Login"

First Time User?

Contact your system administrator to create an account for you.

2 Creating Your First Project

Projects organize your work. Here's how to create one:

  1. Click "+ New Project" button on the main page
  2. Enter project details:
    • Project Name (required)
    • Description
    • Template (Cloud Migration, Software Dev, Infrastructure, or Generic)
  3. Click "Create Project"

Template Benefits:

  • Cloud Migration: Pre-configured for infrastructure projects
  • Software Development: Optimized for agile workflows
  • Infrastructure: Designed for ops teams
  • Generic: Flexible for any project type

3 Adding Your First Issue

Issues track problems, bugs, or tasks that need attention:

  1. Open your project
  2. Click "New Issue" or "Add Issue" button
  3. Fill in the details:
    • Title (required)
    • Description
    • Priority (High/Medium/Low)
    • Category (based on your template)
    • Assign to team member (optional)
  4. Click "Create Issue"

Pro Tip:

Use descriptive titles that clearly state the problem. Good: "Login page returns 500 error". Bad: "Fix bug".

4 Inviting Team Members

Collaborate by inviting your team:

  1. Open your project
  2. Click "Team" button
  3. Click "Invite Team Member"
  4. Enter their email address
  5. Select their role:
    • Admin: Full control
    • Manager: Can invite and manage
    • Member: Can create and edit
    • Viewer: Read-only access
  6. Add optional message
  7. Click "Send Invitation"

The invitee will receive an email and can accept/decline the invitation.
